Levelling up

In Goober Dash, like many other games, requires xp. However, in Goober Dash, to get xp, you need to unlock different skins. The more skins you unlock, the more xp you get. Each level requires a certain amount of xp to level up, and each levels's items give different amounts of xp. The higher the level, the more coins you need to unlock skins, and level up. The cost for each level's items goes:
LEVEL 1: 100 coins, 10xp
LEVEL 2: 135 coins, 11xp
LEVEL 3: 182 coins, 12xp
LEVEL 4: 246 coins, 13xp
LEVEL 5: 332 coins, 15xp
LEVEL 6: 448 coins, 16xp
LEVEL 7: 605 coins, 18xp
LEVEL 8: 817 coins, 19xp
LEVEL 9: 1103 coins, 21xp
LEVEL 10: 1489 coins, 24xp
LEVEL 11: 2000 coins, 26xp
LEVEL 12: 2000 coins, 29xp
LEVEL 13: 2000 coins, 50xp
To get level 14, you need to unlock every single item with coins (excluding paid items from deals) I find that the best way to level up fast is to play on web, as you sometimes get extra rewards by watching an ad. Try fill up the star meter as fast as possible, and when you need to wait for it to refresh, use your gems to refresh it instantly. By only spending your gems on this, you can get more prize rounds for less gems, and faster, causing you to get coins faster and therefore levelling up faster.
